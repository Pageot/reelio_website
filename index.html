QUESTIONS

Stuff you'll ask anyway.

Does it really stay 100% local?

Yes. Zero cloud, zero telemetry, zero account. Everything sits in reelio/index.db on your computer.

Will it eat my CPU?

The FFmpeg worker pool is capped at 2 processes. Scans are iterative, not recursive, stack-safe on huge folder trees, and the UI never blocks.

What if I unplug my external drive?

Nothing, until you ask. Reelio never re-scans on its own, so as long as the drive stays plugged in your clips stay put. The library only changes when you manually trigger a re-scan: at that point Reelio adds any new files it finds at the watched path and removes anything it can no longer reach. Unplug the drive, then run a scan, and those clips disappear from the library. Your video files themselves are never touched.

Mac / Linux version?

Not yet. Reelio is WinUI 3, Windows-native.
